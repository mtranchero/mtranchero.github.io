I am a PhD candidate in Management of Organizations at the Haas School of Business, UC Berkeley. My research explores how the increasing availability of big data reshapes innovation and strategic decision-making. I will be on the academic job market in summer 2023.

In my job market paper, I study how pharmaceutical firms leverage data-driven decision making in their quest for new drugs and the performance implications of false positive data signals.
